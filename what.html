What do I need to do?

When it's your directorate's turn, your Administrative Officer will reach out to each employee in order of seniority.

This includes GS level, which determines what type of workspace you are eligible for, and years of service, which determines the order in which you get to select a workspace.

Who goes first and why?

Within each grade level, employees with a higher number of years of NSF tenure get to choose first, with an exception for a supervisory role vs. a non-supervisory role at the same grade.
