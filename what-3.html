What size workspace am I eligible for?

  • 180 square feet or more
    ADs/DADs, DDs/DDDs, and Supervisory Executives
  • 100-120 square feet
    Nonsupervisory Executives, GS13-15, AD2-5, GS12 Supervisory, GS12 AOs + OSs
  • 72-81 square feet
    GS12 Nonsupervisory, GS1-11, AD1
  • 54 square feet or less
    Students, Fellows, Experts, Consultants, Contractors
    Experts and Consultants can only occupy hoteling spaces. Contractors will only be assigned space based on contractual needs.
