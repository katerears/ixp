Example

Sam, Tim, and Kit are all GS 9.

Sam was at NSF as an IPA for 2 years, and has now been at NSF as a federal employee for 4 years, and is not a supervisor. (6 years total service)

Tim was a contractor for 3 years, then became a federal employee and has 5 years of continuous NSF service, and is not a supervisor. (5 years total service)

Kit has been at NSF as a federal employee for 2 years, and is a supervisor.

Who gets to choose first?

  • Kit gets to choose first. (supervisor)
  • Then, Sam. (6 years)
  • Finally, Tim. (5 years)
